Dessert recipe.

Here's a recipe for a great apple pie. Super simple, super tasty!
All you're going to need are:

Product Quantity
Apples 300g
Flour 1 cup
Butter 200g
Sour cream 100g
Cinnamon 1-2 tablespoons
Baking powder 1 teaspoon

Peel the appleas and cut into small pieces. Fry them on a pan with cinnamon and honey.
Using flour, butter and sour cream make a crust. Then put all the ingredients layer by layer in a baking vessel and bake.
